Frequently Asked Questions — Google Ads for Doctors India

Do Google Ads work for doctors in India?

Yes. Google Ads work exceptionally well for doctors in India because patients actively search for "best dermatologist near me" or "orthopaedic doctor in [city]" before booking. Search intent is high — these are patients actively looking for a doctor right now, not passive social media users.

How much does Google Ads for doctors cost in India?

Google Ads for doctors in India typically cost ₹15,000–₹50,000 per month in ad spend depending on specialty, city, and competition. Free Mind's management fee is separate. Cost-per-lead for most specialties ranges from ₹150 to ₹600 per new patient inquiry.

Which specialties get the best results from Google Ads in India?

Highest ROI specialties for Google Ads in India: dermatology, orthopaedics, gynaecology, dentistry, ophthalmology, hair transplant, IVF/fertility, psychiatry, and weight loss clinics. These have high search volume, clear patient intent, and strong value-per-patient economics.

How long does it take for Google Ads to bring patients to a clinic?

Google Ads can generate patient inquiries within 24–48 hours of going live. Most clinics see their first new patient booking within the first week. Full campaign optimisation — achieving target cost-per-lead — takes 4–8 weeks as the algorithm learns from conversion data.

Can Google Ads target patients in a specific city or area in India?

Yes. Free Mind targets Google Ads campaigns to specific cities, PIN codes, and radius targeting around the clinic. This ensures ad spend is not wasted on patients too far away to book — every click is from a reachable potential patient in your catchment area.
