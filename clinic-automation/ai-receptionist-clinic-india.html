Frequently Asked Questions — AI Receptionist for Clinic India

What is an AI receptionist for a clinic?

An AI receptionist is an automated system that handles patient-facing tasks — booking appointments, answering queries about clinic hours and fees, sending reminders, and managing follow-ups. It operates 24/7 across WhatsApp, website chat, and phone without additional staffing cost.

What tasks can the AI receptionist handle for Indian clinics?

Free Mind's AI receptionist handles: appointment booking and rescheduling via WhatsApp and website, FAQ responses (clinic hours, location, fees, preparation instructions), appointment reminders, post-visit follow-ups, and prescription reminder messages. Complex medical queries are routed to clinic staff.

How much does an AI receptionist cost for a clinic in India?

Free Mind's AI receptionist setup starts from ₹25,000 as a one-time implementation fee with a monthly management retainer from ₹5,000. This is significantly less than the cost of hiring an additional receptionist (₹15,000–₹25,000 per month) who works only 8 hours a day.

Can the AI receptionist make outbound calls to patients?

Yes. Free Mind integrates Koelai AI calling agent with the clinic receptionist system to make automated outbound calls — appointment reminders, follow-up health checks, and lab result notification calls. The AI speaks naturally in Hindi and English.

Is the AI receptionist compliant with Indian patient data laws?

Yes. Free Mind implements patient data handling in compliance with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) 2023. Patient information is stored securely and not shared with third parties. Data retention and deletion policies are configured per clinic requirements.
