Automated Follow-Up Sequences

Every message triggered automatically — personalised for each patient.

💊

Medication Reminders

Daily or weekly WhatsApp reminders based on the prescribed medication schedule. Patients who complete their medication course have better outcomes and higher trust in the doctor.

Post-Visit Health Check-In

Day 3 message: "How are you feeling after your visit, [Name]? If you have any concerns, reply to this message." Simple check-in that catches complications early and builds patient loyalty significantly.

Next Appointment Nudge

Automated reminder 1 week before the suggested follow-up visit with a direct booking link. Patients who receive this reminder are 3x more likely to rebook than patients who don't.

Lab Result Notifications

Automated message when lab results are ready — "Your test results are ready. Please WhatsApp us or book a follow-up appointment to discuss." Reduces incoming calls and speeds up patient care.

Frequently Asked Questions — Patient Follow-Up Automation India

What is patient follow-up automation for clinics?

Patient follow-up automation sends pre-scheduled WhatsApp messages to patients after their clinic visit — medication reminders, recovery check-in questions, next appointment suggestions, and review requests. It replaces manual follow-up calls with automated workflows that run without staff involvement.

What types of follow-up messages can be automated for Indian clinics?

Free Mind automates: post-visit health check-in (day 3), medication reminder (daily/weekly), next appointment nudge (7 days before follow-up), lab result notification, seasonal health check reminder, and Google review request (day 2 after visit).

How does follow-up automation increase clinic revenue?

Automated follow-up increases clinic revenue by improving patient return rates (patients who receive follow-up messages are 3x more likely to rebook), generating Google reviews that improve local SEO and attract new patients, and identifying complications early — reducing missed follow-up visits.

How much does patient follow-up automation cost for an Indian clinic?

Patient follow-up automation setup at Free Mind costs ₹15,000 as a one-time implementation fee. Monthly WhatsApp API message charges are approximately ₹2,000–₹4,000 depending on patient volume. The system pays for itself with a single additional return visit per day.

Can follow-up messages be personalised for each patient?

Yes. Free Mind personalises messages with the patient's name, doctor's name, visit date, and treatment-specific instructions. Diabetes patients receive blood sugar monitoring reminders; orthopaedic patients receive physiotherapy follow-up nudges — tailored messaging for each patient type.
