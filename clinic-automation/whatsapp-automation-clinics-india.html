Frequently Asked Questions — WhatsApp Clinic Automation India

How does WhatsApp appointment reminder automation work for clinics?

Free Mind connects your clinic's appointment system to WhatsApp API. When a patient books, they instantly receive a WhatsApp confirmation. A reminder is automatically sent 24 hours before and 1 hour before the appointment — with an option for the patient to confirm or reschedule directly from the message.

WhatsApp clinic automation setup at Free Mind starts from ₹12,000 as a one-time setup fee. Monthly WhatsApp API message charges are billed at Meta's standard rates (approximately ₹0.50–₹0.80 per conversation). Most clinics spend ₹2,000–₹5,000 per month depending on patient volume.

Can WhatsApp reminders reduce clinic no-shows?

Yes. Automated WhatsApp reminders reduce no-show rates by 40–60% in most Indian clinics. The combination of a 24-hour reminder (gives patients time to reschedule) and a 1-hour reminder (catches last-minute forgetting) is what drives the highest reduction.

Can the WhatsApp bot handle patient queries automatically?

Yes. Free Mind can set up a WhatsApp chatbot that automatically answers common patient questions — clinic hours, location, preparation instructions, fee information — before routing complex queries to the receptionist. This reduces incoming calls by 30–50%.

Is WhatsApp Business API different from regular WhatsApp?

Yes. WhatsApp Business API allows automated messaging, broadcast message templates, and integration with booking systems — features not available on regular WhatsApp or WhatsApp Business app. It is required for proper clinic automation. Free Mind handles the full API setup and compliance.
