Frequently Asked Questions — SEO for Doctors India

What is local SEO for doctors in India?

Local SEO for doctors involves optimising your clinic's online presence to appear in Google Maps and local search results when patients search for your specialty in your city. It includes Google Business Profile optimisation, local keyword targeting, review management, and medical content creation.

How long does SEO take for a clinic in India?

Local SEO for clinics shows measurable results — improved Google Maps ranking, more GBP views, more website visits — within 60–90 days. Strong organic rankings for competitive terms take 4–6 months of consistent SEO work.

What is Google Business Profile optimisation for doctors?

Google Business Profile optimisation involves completing every field in your clinic's Google listing — services, photos, hours, description, appointment link — and actively managing reviews and Q&A. A fully optimised GBP listing significantly increases visibility in Google Maps and local search.

How do online reviews affect SEO for doctors in India?

Google uses review quantity, recency, and rating as ranking signals for local search. A clinic with 50 recent reviews will consistently outrank a clinic with 10 old reviews for the same specialty search. Free Mind implements a systematic review collection strategy via WhatsApp post-visit follow-ups.

Can SEO replace Google Ads for doctors in India?

SEO and Google Ads serve different timelines. SEO takes 3–6 months but then delivers free organic patients indefinitely. Google Ads work immediately but stop when you stop paying. Free Mind recommends running Google Ads while building SEO, then reducing ad spend as organic rankings improve.
