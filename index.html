Ghostwriting
Ghostwriting is the practice of hiring a professional writer to write content — a book, speech, or article — that is published under the client's name. Ghostwriting is entirely legal and has been standard practice for centuries among politicians, celebrities, executives, and authors. The ghostwriter is compensated and assigns all rights to the client. At Free Mind, every ghostwriting engagement is protected by a legally binding NDA and a full rights transfer agreement.
The process starts with a series of structured interviews where the ghostwriter captures your voice, ideas, stories, and perspective. The writer then drafts chapters, shares them for review, and revises based on your feedback. Multiple rounds of revision are included. The result is a manuscript that sounds authentically like you. An NDA is signed before any information is shared.
Free Mind matches clients with ghostwriters who have demonstrated expertise in the client's domain — business, governance, academia, health, or technology. For highly technical subjects, a subject-matter expert reviewer is added to the team to ensure accuracy.

LMS Development
An LMS (Learning Management System) is a digital platform where you host, deliver, and manage online courses. For a coaching institute, an LMS allows learners to access course content anytime, track their own progress, take assessments, and receive certificates — all without requiring physical attendance. It enables institutes to serve hundreds or thousands of learners simultaneously and generate passive income through self-paced course sales.
A standard branded LMS with course management, learner progress tracking, certificate generation, and payment integration takes eight to fourteen weeks depending on the number of courses, integration requirements, and design complexity. A basic version can be launched in four to six weeks.
SCORM (Sharable Content Object Reference Model) is a technical standard that ensures eLearning content works on any compliant LMS. Content built to SCORM standards can be reused, tracked for learner completion, and scored accurately. Free Mind builds SCORM-compliant modules that can be uploaded to any standard LMS including Moodle, TalentLMS, Canvas, and custom platforms.
