Frequently Asked Questions — LMS Development India

What is an LMS and why does a coaching institute need one?

An LMS (Learning Management System) is a digital platform where coaching institutes host, deliver, and manage online courses. It allows learners to access content anytime from any device, track progress, take assessments, and receive certificates — enabling institutes to serve thousands of learners simultaneously and generate passive income through self-paced course sales.

How long does custom LMS development take in India?

A full-featured LMS with course management, learner tracking, certificate generation, and payment integration takes 8-14 weeks. A basic version with essential features can launch in 4-6 weeks. Timeline depends on number of courses, integration requirements, and design complexity.

What is SCORM compliance and why does it matter?

SCORM (Sharable Content Object Reference Model) is a technical standard that ensures eLearning content works on any compliant LMS, can track learner completion, and score assessments accurately. Free Mind builds SCORM-compliant LMS platforms and content modules that meet this standard.

Can the LMS support multiple courses and student batches?

Yes. Free Mind builds multi-course, multi-batch LMS platforms. Each course can have its own modules, pricing, assessments, and certificates. Admins manage everything from a centralized dashboard with batch-specific access control and student management.

What payment gateways are supported in the LMS?

Free Mind integrates Razorpay and PayU for Indian payment processing — supporting UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, and wallets. One-time purchase, subscription, and batch-based pricing models are all supported.
