What is an AI calling agent and how can it help my startup?

An AI calling agent is an automated voice system that handles inbound and outbound calls 24/7. For startups, it means never missing a lead, automating follow-ups, and scaling customer communication without hiring a large team. Our Koelai AI supports 100+ languages and integrates with your CRM.

How long does it take to automate business processes?

A typical business automation engagement takes 4–8 weeks from discovery to deployment. We begin with a process audit, identify the highest-impact workflows, build the solution, and train your team. Simple automations can be live in 2 weeks.

Do you build MVPs for investor demos?

Yes. We design and develop MVPs specifically for investor demonstrations and early user validation. Our MVP engagements deliver a functional, demo-ready product in 6–12 weeks depending on complexity.

Can you help me build a personal brand as a founder?

Absolutely. Personal branding includes LinkedIn optimization, thought leadership content, media pitching, podcast appearances, and ghostwritten articles. Your story is your strongest business asset — we help you tell it to the right audience.
