Frequently Asked Questions — Business Automation India

What is business automation consulting in India?

Business automation consulting involves mapping a company's repetitive manual processes — lead follow-up, invoice generation, appointment booking, reporting — and replacing them with automated workflows. Free Mind uses tools like Zapier, Make, n8n, WhatsApp API, and AI agents to design and deploy these systems for Indian startups and SMEs.

Which business processes can be automated?

Free Mind commonly automates: lead capture and CRM entry, WhatsApp follow-up sequences, appointment reminders, invoice and payment workflows, customer onboarding, social media scheduling, sales pipeline updates, and monthly reporting. Almost any repeatable task is a candidate for automation.

How much does business automation cost in India?

Business automation projects range from ₹25,000 for a single workflow (e.g., WhatsApp lead follow-up) to ₹2,00,000+ for a full operations overhaul across multiple departments. Free Mind provides a fixed-scope quote after a 30-minute discovery call.

How long does automation implementation take?

A single automation workflow takes 1–2 weeks to design, build, and test. A full business automation project covering multiple departments typically takes 4–8 weeks including discovery, build, testing, and handover training.

Do you use AI in business automation?

Yes. Free Mind integrates AI agents and Koelai AI calling technology into automation workflows for tasks requiring natural language — lead qualification, appointment booking calls, customer query handling, and intelligent report generation. AI automation handles what rule-based tools cannot.
