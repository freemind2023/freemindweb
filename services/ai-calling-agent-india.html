Frequently Asked Questions — AI Calling Agent India

What is an AI calling agent?

An AI calling agent is software that makes and receives phone calls using artificial intelligence to conduct natural spoken conversations. Unlike IVR systems that play pre-recorded prompts, an AI calling agent listens, understands natural language, and responds contextually — handling lead qualification, appointment booking, and customer service 24/7.

How is Koelai AI different from an IVR system?

An IVR plays pre-recorded prompts and routes calls by button press. Koelai AI conducts full two-way spoken conversations — it listens, understands context, handles objections, answers complex questions, and escalates to a human when needed. It replaces the need for human receptionists and call center agents.

Which Indian languages does Koelai AI support?

Koelai supports 100+ languages including all major Indian regional languages: Hindi, Marathi, Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, Kannada, Malayalam, Gujarati, Punjabi, and more. It auto-detects the caller's language and responds accordingly without any manual configuration per call.

What industries use AI calling agents in India?
