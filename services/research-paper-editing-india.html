What Research Paper Editing Includes

From raw draft to submission-ready manuscript — every step covered.

Structural Review

Abstract, introduction, literature review, methodology, results, discussion, and conclusion reviewed for logical flow, consistency, and adherence to journal structure requirements.

Frequently Asked Questions — Research Paper Editing India

What does research paper editing include?

Research paper editing at Free Mind includes language editing (grammar, syntax, clarity, academic tone), structural editing (abstract through conclusion), reference formatting (APA, MLA, Chicago, Vancouver), plagiarism check, and journal-specific formatting for your target publication.

How do you choose the right journal for submission in India?

Free Mind matches your paper to suitable Scopus or Web of Science indexed journals based on subject area, scope, impact factor, acceptance rate, and average turnaround time. We provide a ranked shortlist of 3–5 target journals with submission guidelines for each.

How long does research paper editing take?

A standard research paper (5,000–8,000 words) takes 3–5 business days for language and structural editing. Journal submission formatting and cover letter preparation add 1–2 business days. Rush turnaround in 48 hours is available for an additional fee.

Do you handle plagiarism checks for research papers?

Yes. All papers go through a plagiarism check using industry-standard tools. Free Mind provides a similarity report and, if needed, rewrites flagged sections to bring similarity below the journal's acceptable threshold — typically under 15% for Scopus-indexed journals.

Do you edit papers for Indian PhD students and professors?

Yes. Free Mind edits research papers for PhD scholars, professors, post-doctoral researchers, and independent academics across all disciplines — engineering, medicine, social sciences, management, law, and humanities. Subject-expert reviewers are matched to your specific field.
