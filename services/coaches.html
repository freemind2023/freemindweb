Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to build a custom LMS in India?

A custom LMS in India typically costs ₹2,00,000 to ₹15,00,000 depending on features, user capacity, integrations, and design complexity. We build LMS platforms with course management, assessments, certificates, and payment gateway integration.

What is SCORM compliance and do I need it?

SCORM is a technical standard for eLearning content ensuring your courses work across different LMS platforms. If you plan to sell courses on third-party platforms or deliver corporate training, SCORM compliance is essential. We develop fully SCORM-compliant content packages.

Can you design a curriculum for national accreditation?

Yes. We design curricula aligned with NAAC, NBA, AICTE, and UGC frameworks. Our curriculum design follows Bloom's Taxonomy and Outcome-Based Education principles required by most accreditation bodies.

How can an AI podcast help my coaching institute?

An AI podcast lets your institute create consistent, professional audio content at scale — building brand authority, attracting students through podcast SEO, and creating a shareable educational content library without a full production team.
