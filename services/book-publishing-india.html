Frequently Asked Questions — Book Publishing India

How long does book publishing take in India?

Book publishing in India takes 6–10 weeks from final manuscript to live on Amazon. Editing takes 2–3 weeks, design 1–2 weeks, ISBN registration 1 week, and Amazon KDP setup 3–5 days. Academic or translated editions may take 2–4 weeks longer.

How much does self-publishing a book cost in India?

Self-publishing in India costs ₹20,000 to ₹1,50,000 depending on manuscript length, editing depth, cover design, and distribution scope. Free Mind uses modular pricing — you pay only for the services you need.

Do I retain copyright when self-publishing?

Yes. Authors retain 100% copyright and intellectual property rights. Free Mind is a service provider, not a publisher claiming rights. You also keep 60–70% royalties on Amazon KDP versus 8–15% with traditional publishers.

Will my book be available in physical bookstores?

Yes. Through IngramSpark distribution, your book can be ordered by physical bookstores and libraries worldwide. Print-on-demand means no minimum print run is required.

What is the difference between self-publishing and traditional publishing?

Traditional publishing means a publisher funds production but takes most royalties (8–15%) and creative control. Self-publishing means you fund production and retain full rights with higher royalties (60–70%). Free Mind delivers traditional-quality results with self-publishing economics.
