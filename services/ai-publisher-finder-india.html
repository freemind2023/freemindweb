How AI Publisher Matching Works

Four steps from manuscript to matched publisher shortlist.

📖

Manuscript Analysis

Submit your manuscript summary, genre, word count, target reader profile, and comps (comparable titles). The AI extracts the key acquisition signals publishers use to evaluate fit.

🤖

Publisher Database Matching

AI matches your manuscript against 500+ publisher profiles — genre preferences, recent acquisitions, submission guidelines, advance ranges, and royalty structures. Publishers closed to submissions are filtered out automatically.

📊

Fit Score & Ranked Shortlist

Receive a ranked shortlist of 10–15 publishers with fit scores, submission tips tailored to each publisher's preferences, and estimated response times. Know which publisher to approach first.

📩

Query Letter & Submission Support

Optional: Free Mind writes a publisher-ready query letter and synopsis tailored to your top 3 targets. Submission support available for managing responses, follow-ups, and negotiations.

🌏

Indian + International Coverage

Database covers major Indian English publishers (Penguin India, HarperCollins India, Rupa, Westland, Hachette India), regional language publishers, and international publishers — UK, US, and Australian houses accepting Indian manuscripts.

🔄

Self-Publishing Fallback

If traditional publishing isn't the right path, Free Mind recommends a self-publishing plan with projected timelines, costs, and royalty comparisons — so you make the decision with full information.

Frequently Asked Questions — AI Publisher Finder India

What is an AI publisher finder?

An AI publisher finder analyses your manuscript — genre, subject, writing style, word count, and target audience — and matches it with publishers most likely to accept it. Free Mind's PublishMatch AI covers 500+ Indian and international publishers and ranks them by fit score, acceptance rate, and average response time.

How does AI find the right publisher for my book?

PublishMatch AI analyses your manuscript summary and metadata against a database of publisher preferences, submission guidelines, recent acquisitions, and genre specialisations. It produces a ranked shortlist with fit scores, submission tips, and direct submission links for each publisher on your list.

Is AI publisher matching available for regional language books?

Yes. Free Mind's publisher database includes major Hindi, Marathi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Bengali, and Malayalam publishers in addition to English-language publishers. Regional language authors can get matched to vernacular publishers with active acquisition programmes.

How is AI publisher matching better than manual research?

Manual publisher research takes weeks of reading submission guidelines, checking social media, and tracking down contact details. PublishMatch AI completes this analysis in minutes, surfaces publishers you'd never find through Google, and filters out closed publishers — saving months of trial-and-error rejections.

Can it help me decide between self-publishing and traditional publishing?

Yes. PublishMatch AI can be run in two modes — traditional publisher matching and hybrid/self-publishing assessment — to compare projected income, timeline, and creative control for both paths. Free Mind consultants interpret the results and recommend the optimal publishing strategy for your specific book and goals.
