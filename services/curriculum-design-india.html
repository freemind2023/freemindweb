Frequently Asked Questions — Curriculum Design India

What is curriculum design for online courses?

Curriculum design for online courses is the structured process of mapping learning objectives, sequencing content modules, choosing delivery methods, and creating assessments so learners achieve defined outcomes. Free Mind uses the ADDIE framework — Analyse, Design, Develop, Implement, Evaluate — for every curriculum project.

What is the ADDIE framework in curriculum design?

ADDIE stands for Analyse, Design, Develop, Implement, and Evaluate. It is the industry-standard instructional design model used to build effective, learner-centred courses. Free Mind applies ADDIE to ensure every curriculum is structured around measurable outcomes, not just topic coverage.

How long does curriculum design take in India?

A complete curriculum for a 20–30 hour online course takes 4–8 weeks depending on subject complexity and number of modules. A short 5–10 hour course takes 2–4 weeks. SME review rounds and revision cycles are built into the timeline.

Will the curriculum work on my LMS platform?

Yes. Free Mind delivers SCORM-compatible course files that work on all major LMS platforms including Moodle, Teachable, Thinkific, LearnDash, TalentLMS, and custom-built systems. We can also help configure and deploy the LMS itself.

Do you design curriculum for coaching institutes in India?

Yes. Free Mind designs curriculum for online coaching institutes, offline training centres converting to digital, corporate L&D teams, and edtech startups across India. We handle subject-matter expert interviews, content structure, quiz design, and full assessment frameworks.
