Frequently Asked Questions

How do IAS officers publish their memoir or governance book?

IAS and civil service officers publish memoirs through: drafting or ghostwriting the manuscript, editorial review, ISBN registration, cover design, printing, and distribution. We manage this entire process with a strict NDA so your authorship is fully preserved.

Can my governance memoir reach audiences in regional languages?

Yes. We translate memoirs and governance books into Hindi, Marathi, Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, Gujarati, and other regional languages so your work reaches the communities and districts you served.

How do I build a public intellectual presence after retirement?

Publishing a book is the most powerful first step. Beyond that, we arrange podcast features, ghostwrite op-eds and LinkedIn articles, and develop a personal brand strategy that positions you as a public intellectual, speaker, or policy advisor.
