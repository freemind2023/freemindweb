Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to publish a book in India?

Publishing costs in India range from ₹20,000 to ₹1,50,000 depending on manuscript length, editing rounds, cover design, ISBN, and print vs. digital distribution. We offer transparent, service-by-service pricing with no hidden fees.

How long does it take to publish a book?

A standard project takes 6–10 weeks from manuscript to live on Amazon. Rush timelines of 3–4 weeks are possible for manuscripts requiring minimal editing.

Is ghostwriting kept confidential?

Yes. All ghostwriting is covered by an NDA. Full authorship rights belong to you. We never claim credit for or disclose ghostwritten work.

How do I find the right publisher for my book?

Use our free PublishMatch AI tool. Answer a few questions about your book's genre, language, and goals — our AI instantly matches you with ideal publishers from 1000+ in our database.

Can you translate my book into regional Indian languages?

Yes. We translate into Hindi, Marathi, Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, Gujarati, Kannada, Malayalam, and other languages. Each translated edition can receive its own ISBN.
