Frequently Asked Questions — Ghostwriting India

What is ghostwriting and is it legal in India?

Ghostwriting is hiring a professional writer to write content published under the client's name. It is entirely legal and has been standard practice for centuries among politicians, executives, celebrities, and authors worldwide. At Free Mind, every engagement is protected by a legally binding NDA and a full rights transfer agreement.

How does the ghostwriting process work at Free Mind?

The process starts with structured interview sessions (video or audio) where the ghostwriter captures your voice, ideas, stories, and perspective. The writer then drafts chapters progressively and shares for your review. Multiple revision rounds are included until the manuscript sounds authentically like you.

How long does ghostwriting a book take in India?

A full-length book (50,000-80,000 words) typically takes 3-6 months depending on the number of interview sessions, client availability for reviews, and revision depth. Shorter projects like thought leadership articles or speeches take 1-4 weeks.

Can I be listed as the sole author of a ghostwritten book?

Yes, completely. You are the author. The ghostwriter works anonymously under a full NDA and rights transfer agreement. Free Mind never claims credit for or discloses ghostwritten work under any circumstances.

Will the ghostwriter understand my specific industry?

Yes. Free Mind matches clients with ghostwriters who have demonstrated expertise in the client's domain — business, governance, academia, health, or technology. For highly technical subjects, a subject-matter expert reviewer is added to the team to ensure accuracy.
