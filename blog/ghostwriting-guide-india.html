Ghostwriting Services India 2026 — How It Works, Cost & How to Find the Right Ghostwriter

Ghostwriting is one of the oldest professions in publishing — and one of the least understood in India. This guide covers everything: what ghostwriting actually involves, what it costs in the Indian market, how to find a trustworthy ghostwriter, and how to protect yourself with the right contract. If you have a book, memoir, or speech in mind but aren't sure about writing it yourself, this is your starting point.

What is Ghostwriting?

Ghostwriting is when a professional writer creates content — a book, memoir, speech, article, or script — on behalf of another person, who then publishes it under their own name. The ghostwriter is paid for their work and agrees, typically through a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA), not to publicly claim authorship.

Ghostwriting is not fraud. It is a professional service that has been used by heads of state, business leaders, celebrities, and public figures for centuries. Many of India's most prominent business books, political memoirs, and motivational titles have been ghostwritten or co-written with professional writers.

The named author provides the ideas, experiences, knowledge, and direction. The ghostwriter provides the writing craft, structure, research depth, and publishing expertise. Both contribute essential value.

What Can Be Ghostwritten in India?

  • Books and memoirs — The most common type. A professional ghostwriter interviews you, researches your story, and writes a full manuscript under your name.
  • Business books — Founders, consultants, and executives who want to establish authority in their field hire ghostwriters to turn their expertise into a publishable book.
  • IAS/IPS officer memoirs — Retired civil servants with decades of service often want to document their experience. Our publishing service for government officers includes ghostwriting with full NDA protection.
  • Speeches and keynotes — Corporate speeches, TEDx talks, wedding speeches, and political addresses.
  • Academic and research content — Professors who need help structuring and writing books for an academic audience.
  • Social media and content — Founders and coaches who want consistent LinkedIn, Instagram, or blog content written in their voice.

The Ghostwriting Process — Step by Step

Here is how a professional ghostwriting engagement works from start to finish:

1

Discovery Call and Scope Agreement

You and the ghostwriter discuss the project — what you want to write, who the audience is, the approximate length, and the timeline. A scope document and fee agreement are signed before any work begins.

2

NDA Signing

A Non-Disclosure Agreement is signed. This legally binds the ghostwriter to confidentiality — they cannot disclose the project, claim authorship, or use your material for any other purpose. All intellectual property transfers to you.

3

Interviews and Research

The ghostwriter conducts structured interviews with you — typically 4–10 sessions for a full book — to capture your voice, stories, knowledge, and perspective. For memoirs, this is where your life gets translated into material.

4

Outline and Structure

Before writing begins, the ghostwriter presents a chapter-by-chapter outline. You review, revise, and approve it. This ensures the final manuscript reflects your vision — not the ghostwriter's default structure.

5

Drafting

The ghostwriter writes the manuscript chapter by chapter, sharing drafts with you regularly for feedback. Most professional ghostwriters include 2–3 revision rounds per chapter.

6

Final Delivery and Publishing

Once the manuscript is approved, it is delivered to you as the sole author. If you are self-publishing, this manuscript goes into editing, cover design, and formatting before Amazon distribution. Free Mind handles the entire publishing pipeline after ghostwriting if required.

How to Find a Ghostwriter in India

Finding the right ghostwriter is about more than finding someone who can write. You are looking for someone who can write in your voice, understand your audience, and manage a multi-month project professionally. Here is what to look for:

  • Samples in your genre. A ghostwriter who has written business books may not be the right fit for a memoir. Ask for samples closest to what you want to write.
  • Interview process. Good ghostwriters invest time in understanding you before writing. Be wary of anyone who wants to start immediately without deep discovery sessions.
  • NDA first, always. Never share your story, ideas, or unpublished material without a signed NDA. This is non-negotiable.
  • Milestone payments. Pay in stages — discovery, outline, first draft, final delivery — never all upfront.
  • References or testimonials. Professional ghostwriters can provide references from past clients (confidentially) or point to published books they worked on.

Is Ghostwriting Legal and Ethical in India?

Ghostwriting is 100% legal in India. There is no law prohibiting it, and it is not considered fraud or deception in publishing. The practice is widely accepted in Indian corporate, political, and literary circles.

Ethically, the argument is straightforward: you own your ideas, experiences, and knowledge. You are hiring a specialist to help you express them in writing — the same way you hire a CA to file your taxes or an architect to design your home. The ideas are yours. The professional helps with execution.

The only context where ghostwriting is genuinely problematic is academic work (essays, theses, dissertations) submitted for evaluation. This guide covers professional publishing only.

Ghostwriting vs Co-Authorship — What is the Difference?

In ghostwriting, only your name appears on the cover. In co-authorship, both names appear. The choice depends on your preference and the level of the writer's public recognition.

Most business leaders, officers, and coaches prefer ghostwriting for privacy. Some choose co-authorship when the writer's name adds credibility (e.g., a well-known journalist or academic co-writing with a public figure).

Yes. The book is published under your name only. The ghostwriter's name does not appear anywhere. You retain 100% of the royalties from Amazon KDP and all other platforms.

A short business book (20,000 words) takes 3–4 months. A full memoir (60,000–80,000 words) takes 5–8 months. The timeline includes discovery interviews, research, drafting, and revision rounds.
