# Business Goals — Free Mind Consultancy

## Primary Objectives

1. **Increase organic traffic** by 30% quarter-over-quarter
2. **Improve lead generation** from organic search
3. **Rank on page 1** for core service keywords
4. **Build topical authority** in consulting and digital marketing niches

## Target Audience

- Small business owners in India looking for digital marketing services
- Entrepreneurs seeking consulting for growth
- Professionals looking for personal branding help
- Clinic and healthcare professionals needing automation

## Service Verticals to Optimize For

1. Meme Marketing
2. Clinic Automation
3. Book Publishing
4. KoelAI (AI Solutions)
5. Founders Consulting
6. LMS (Learning Management)
7. Personal Branding
8. Social Media Management
9. Website Development

## KPIs to Track

- Organic sessions (monthly)
- Keyword rankings for target terms
- Click-through rate from search
- Pages per session
- Conversion rate (form submissions, contact clicks)
- Domain authority trend
- Indexed pages count

## Content Strategy Priorities

- Service landing pages (one per vertical)
- Blog posts targeting long-tail keywords
- Case studies and testimonials
- FAQ content for each service
- Local SEO content for India market

## Constraints

- Budget: Organic-first approach, minimal paid spend
- Frequency: Monthly comprehensive review, weekly quick checks
- Deployment: All changes reviewed manually before going live
