About Us Pune, Maharashtra

We Help Ideas Become Books, Businesses, and Brands

Free Mind Consultancy is a Pune-based consultancy that helps authors publish their first book, founders scale their digital presence, and government officers share their story with the world. Everything we do is rooted in one belief: your ideas deserve to be heard.

Who We Are

Founded by Aryan Ahire in Pune, Free Mind Consultancy started with a simple observation: talented people — authors, coaches, civil servants, entrepreneurs — had valuable knowledge but no reliable path to share it. Publishers were gatekeepers. Digital marketing agencies were expensive and generic. There was no trusted partner for someone who had something real to say.

We built Free Mind to be that partner. Today, we work with authors across India, founders scaling startups, government officers documenting decades of service, and clinics growing their patient base through smart digital marketing.

What We Do

Our services fall into three main areas:

  • Publishing services — Book publishing, ISBN registration, ghostwriting, and publishing for government officers. We handle everything from manuscript to Amazon — you keep 100% of your rights.
  • Digital marketing — Meme marketing for startups, Google Ads, SEO, and content strategy for founders and growing businesses.
  • Clinic automation — Google Ads and AI tools for doctors and clinics — appointment bookings, patient acquisition, and reducing no-shows.
Our publishing approach is different: we don't take any share of your royalties. You pay once for the service, you own the book, and every rupee from sales goes directly to you. Our step-by-step guide to publishing in India explains exactly how the process works.

Our Products

Beyond services, we build AI-powered tools for Indian businesses:

  • Koelai AI — An AI calling agent that makes and receives phone calls in Hindi and English, 24/7. Used by clinics, real estate firms, and ed-tech companies for appointment reminders, lead follow-ups, and customer surveys.
  • PublishMatch AI — A tool that matches your manuscript with Indian publishers who are actively accepting submissions in your genre.

Who We Work With

We serve five main groups, each with dedicated services:

  • Authors — First-time authors who want to publish without giving up rights or royalties. We handle the full process.
  • Founders and startups — Growing businesses that need digital marketing, content, and growth strategy without agency-level pricing.
  • Government officers (IAS, IPS, IFS) — Civil servants with decades of experience who want to document and share their stories — confidentially, with full NDA coverage.
  • Professors and academics — Researchers who want to publish educational books, guides, or textbooks for the Indian market.
  • Coaches and trainers — Coaches who want to build authority through a published book, a content strategy, or both.

Why Free Mind?

Three things set us apart from other publishing services and digital agencies in India:

  • No royalty cuts. Traditional publishers take 85–92% of royalties. We don't take any. You pay a one-time service fee, and every sale is yours.
  • End-to-end accountability. We don't hand you off to freelancers. Your project is managed in-house, with clear timelines and one point of contact.
  • India-first expertise. We know the Indian publishing ecosystem — from ISBN registration with the Government of India to Amazon KDP distribution, from WhatsApp-first marketing to Pune-based reader communities. We don't apply generic advice.
