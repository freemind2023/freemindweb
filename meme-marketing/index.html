Quick Answer

What is meme marketing? Meme marketing is the practice of using internet memes, viral humor, trending formats, and relatable cultural references to promote a brand on social media. It generates organic reach because people share memes voluntarily — no paid boosting required. In India, meme-based content consistently outperforms polished brand creatives in engagement and reach.
